Crocotile 3D



I use Crocotile 3D to create low-poly 3D models quickly and intuitively. The tile-based modeling system allows me to draw and build directly in 3D space, making the process more fluid and giving me complete control over every detail.

With this approach, I can develop lightweight and optimized objects and scenarios, perfect for games that require a stylized look and efficient performance.
For me, Crocotile is a tool that facilitates the connection between design and functionality, allowing me to translate my concepts into projects in a practical and faithful way.
